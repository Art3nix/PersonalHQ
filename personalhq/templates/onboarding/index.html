How would you like to begin?

Life HQ is driven by your identities. You can let our AI build a custom system for you, or construct it manually.

1
Identity-Based Change

True behavior change is identity change. The goal is not to read a book; the goal is to become a reader. When you start with who you want to be, the actions naturally follow.

2
Systems Over Goals

You do not rise to the level of your goals; you fall to the level of your systems. Life HQ is designed to translate your abstract identities into concrete daily systems (Habits, Time Buckets, Journals).

3
The Blank Slate Warning

If you choose to start with a Blank Workspace, you are responsible for building this system from scratch. We highly recommend letting the AI build your first iteration to see how the ecosystem functions together.
