Clear Your Mind

The goal right now is not to write a masterpiece. It is simply to move swirling thoughts out of your mind and onto the page. Do not worry about editing or sounding perfect. Just let the words flow so you can find some peace.

Be Kind To Yourself

Your journal is a safe space, not a courtroom. If you had a tough day, write it down, but do it with self-compassion. You are just reflecting on exactly where you are right now so you can make tomorrow a little bit brighter.
